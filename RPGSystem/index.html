What is RPGSystem?

RPGSystem is a designed for writing works of fiction based on an RPG-like system.


How does it work?

First, a system (novel or book) is made. Within this system, characters can be added, who can then have skills, stats, and items. Then, scenarios can be played out by adding characters to a scene and then performing actions like attacks, or using items.


Time can also play a role in the scenario, where a certain time can be registered and the acompanying regenerated health, stamina, mana, etc. is added based on their restoration values.
