What is DoorsOS?

DoorsOS is a game based on the Windows 95 and 98 operating systems. The point of the game is to hack into companies to destroy, modify, or steal information through a variety of methods.


How do you play?

To play the game, you use tools and doors like you would in a normal desktop environment. The player can use prebuilt tools for hacking, or write their own. Higher difficulties give you less to work with and harder challenges, however the bonus is that you know how the programs work, and can automate tasks.


There is also a multiplayer mode in which teams must hack eachothers' computers or team up against a large corporation in order to discover and exploit vulnerabilities. Different missions are compiled procedurally, so having the same mission more than once is unlikely.
