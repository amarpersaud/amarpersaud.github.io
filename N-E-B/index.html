What is N.E.B.?

N.E.B. is a project designed to create neuroevolutionary "cells" that evolve to best survive in the environment.


What is Neuroevolution?

Neuroevolution is using genetic evolution to decide the weights, biases, and or connections between the neurons of a neural network.


What is Genetic Evolution?

Genetic evolution is the use of DNA, combined from two parents to create a child. The child's DNA is a combination of the two parents'. It is similar to both parents' DNA, but not identical to either. Mutation also occurs at a fixed chance, usually below 5% probability. Both factors contribute to genetic diversity, and allow for varying cells to evolve. The top performing cells are selected to be bred, and the bottom performing cells die off. cells will only mate with cells of the same species, or the cells with somewhat similar DNA (in length and in values), and will choose mates that have high performances.
